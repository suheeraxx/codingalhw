Aaron Warner

Aaron Warner Anderson is the main male character from the "Shatter Me" Series by the popular Iranian writer Tahereh Mafi. He is the Cheif Commander and Regent of Sector 45 in North America. He is described having golden blonde hair and pale emerald eyes. He is one of the most anticipated and popular book character of all time. Shatter Me series is a dystopian and fantasy book, famous among the teenagers and young adults.
